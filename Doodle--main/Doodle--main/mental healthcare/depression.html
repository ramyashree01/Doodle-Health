Anger


Introduction Patient Health Questionnaire (PHQ-9) is a self-report tool used to understand the intensity of depressive symptoms. It consists of 9 questions and takes around 5 minutes to complete. Developed by Spitzer, Kroenke and colleagues in 1999, this scale has been widely used across the globe to measure and assess depression.
Instructions
In this self-report questionnaire, there are no right or wrong answers. You have to read each question carefully and answer them accurately according to how you feel. Answer the questions keeping this in mind, “In the past 2 weeks, how often have you been bothered by any of the following problems?”

You are talking to someone and they don't answer you

Being joked about or teased

Being singled out for a correction, while the actions of others go unnoticed

Someone makes a mistake and blames it on you

Someone sticks his or her nose into an argument between you and someone else
